Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Toolproof (ABN 18 878 662 154) is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit toolproof.com.au, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@toolproof.com.au.

What information we collect

We collect two types of information through this website:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on the site, referring URL, browser and device type. This is collected automatically via cookies if you consent.
  • Contact information — if you call or email us directly, we collect the information you provide (name, email address, phone number) in order to respond to your enquiry.

We do not collect sensitive information and we do not collect information from children.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site. We only set non-essential cookies after you have given your consent via the banner on this site.

  • Google Analytics — measures site traffic and behaviour. Data is processed by Google LLC in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out at any time using Google's opt-out tool.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — measures the effectiveness of our advertising on Meta platforms. Data is processed by Meta Platforms Inc. in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can manage your ad preferences in your Facebook Ad settings.
  • Google Tag Manager — a tag management system used to deploy the above tools. GTM itself does not collect personal data.

We implement Google Consent Mode v2, which means Google tags only activate after you have accepted cookies. If you decline, only anonymous, cookieless signals are sent.

How we use your information

  • To understand how our website is used and improve it
  • To measure the reach and effectiveness of our marketing
  • To respond to enquiries you send us directly

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data storage and retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics before automatic deletion. Contact information provided directly to us is retained for as long as necessary to manage the relationship and comply with our legal obligations.

Data collected via Google and Meta may be transferred to and stored on servers outside Australia. Both providers are signatories to international data transfer frameworks and maintain appropriate safeguards.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for cookie-based tracking at any time using the link in the footer
  • Make a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy has been breached

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@toolproof.com.au.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
